Welcome To Valor International

Our Philosophy is to promote a system of values that focus on honesty, integrity, and good sportsmanship. These are brought forth in an individual and team environment that encourages goal-setting, hard work, personal commitment, and camaraderie through the sport of volleyball.

Threat to Youth Sports

Valor believes that every young child/teenager should be offered the opportunity to develop skills that will eneable them to excell and complete successfully at every level. At Valor, we do our best to find ways to keep cost as low as possible and work within our community to find and cultivate relationships with individuals and business to help suppotrt our programs. Our goal is to help families/athletes have the gift of competitive sport and community service through financial support. This is an exciting time for Valor and we would love to expand our programs to those that simply cannot afford it. Together, we can make this happen.

The average child spends less than 3 years playing a sport, having to quit by age 11. Cost is the issue. 33.4% of kids ages 6-12 from homes with $25,000 or less were physically inactive, compared to just 9.9% of kids from $100,000+ homes.
