Coach Bela da Silva

Club Director/Head Coach

Co-Founder

Native Brazilian from Belo Horizonte, Bela began coaching volleyball at 18. Besides playing, Bela knew that her passion was to coach. She coached/trained several Club teams in Brazil. In 2014, as a student-athlete, she received her Bachelor's in Business Administration and pursues her Master's in Sports Psychology/Coaching.

• Miami Elite & Miami Select, Miami, FL

I am honored and exceedingly proud to be a volleyball coach! My father played professional soccer in Brazil, and since the age of 4, I remember watching him play. My dearest wish and desire were to be on that field and play with him. From that early age, my love for sports was above everything. Volleyball came into my life as a great opportunity. That opportunity developed and shaped the woman I have become today. An offer was made to play College volleyball in the United States when I was 17. It was the scariest moment of my life! Moving to the United-States, on my own, not speaking a single word of English. The biggest challenge I had ever faced. Playing volleyball in college was the experience I needed. It influenced my outlook on life. I graduated in 2014 with my Business Degree. Throughout my years of coaching volleyball, I gained fond memories of every place I have been. Every player I ever had the opportunity and honor to coach. What makes me even more proud of coaching? To see the growth of every young athlete that came aboard, with loads of fear and concerns regarding their choice to play sports, especially volleyball. It took me back to how I felt in my first volleyball practice - scared and intimidated. As a coach, my dedication is to give a positive, unforgettable experience - an exciting first step into the competitive world. My commitment and devotion has helped many players further their careers at colleges and overseas. Helped players develop leadership skills that they can apply to other avenues in their life. One thing for sure - through all the fun, silliness, anger, laughs, struggles and scuffles, cries on and off-court, meetings with parents, trophy presentations, victory laps, and first-place awards - this IS the best job in the world! Volleyball is a thrilling ride of life and spirit!
Allow me to share this passion with you all !

Bela Silva
