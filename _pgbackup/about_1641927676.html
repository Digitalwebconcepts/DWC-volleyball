About Us

Valor International wants to be the place where you find what you are looking for when you think of volleyball. Encompass the thrill of the sport or embrace the most challenging version of yourself. Our staff, Coach Rosie Rivera and Coach Isabela da Silva, have been coaching for over 10 years.Sharing the same life goal, to grow every day, and continue learning. We encourage our players to embrace their valor and always be the best version of themselves. We had the chance to play this sport our entire lives. We still do. Volleyball gave us unforgettable memories, forged forever-friends, families, places we could call home, and the primary purpose of life. No matter the hardships of life, we can choose which way we want to go. Volleyball, like any sport, will show that having a purpose in life and trusting your instincts helps you realize your importance in this world. At Valor, every step we take is part of a process. We want everyone to share their goals and purpose so we can have the opportunity to grow together and achieve our success. We want to focus on developing players and ensuring that everyone understands and is capable of all aspects of volleyball. We have coached many athletes who play at the highest and most competitive divisions in the United States. We have trained professional athletes for their volleyball seasons globally - from the beach to indoor volleyball. Aim and goal are to provide a high degree of technical training for players on three levels: Body, Mind, and Heart. We train and coach any player that wants to get ready for high school and college careers and expose athletes to collegiate volleyball programs through recruiting efforts. We have no aspirations of growing into a sizable club with numerous teams in every age group. Limiting the number of Valor International teams allows us to focus on details while providing the highest quality club volleyball experience possible. Valor can assure that our extraordinary coaches are ready to instruct, guide, and support every player to grow with great opportunity and enjoyment of the game.

Read more about our trainers and co-founders by visiting Our Trainers
